Did you know...?
  • The first Portuguese flag had a blue cross on a white background — symbol of the faith of the first king, Afonso Henriques.
  • Portugal has the oldest borders in Europe, practically unchanged since 1297!
  • The Portuguese Empire was the world’s first global empire and lasted more than 500 years.
